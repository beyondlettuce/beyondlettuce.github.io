What can we grow for you?

Our mission at Beyond Lettuce is provide chefs in the DC and N. VA with what you need, when you need it. When that malabar spinach you fell in love with is no longer available from your local market, contact us to grow it for you. Let us be your local garden.

Our Farm

Our farm remains focused on those harder-to-find items, heirloom varieties, and heritage greens. We want to preserve those local cultural food traditions we cherish. Our farm is indoors in a warehouse setting using a combination of hydroponic and other indoor systems. We limit ourselves to produce that adapts well to an indoor environment and supplement our light sources with the best grow light technology we can get. Our plant nutrients are developed in-house using worm compost and other organic sources.
