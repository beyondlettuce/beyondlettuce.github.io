First up... Microgreens! These little goodies are packing a massive amount of nutrition in a tiny package. You need a better salad than a bowl of lettuce with a few toppings. Use microgreens to add nutrition, color, and flavor. Add them to sandwiches and soups. Put them on tacos, eggs, and pizza. We've even made a few cocktails with them. Microgreens are better than supplements and vitamin pills to get the nutrition you need, the taste you want and the look to show off.

About Beyond Lettuce

Beyond Lettuce grows indoors in the heart of Tysons Corner, VA. Our location was chosen specifically for hyper-local access to specialty products like microgreens. Many of our most popular products have a tight harvest window to be at their best. To ensure you get the best product, we offer pickup by appointment. Let us know what you are looking for using the form below. If you are looking for something not listed, let us know that as well as many of our products are grown by request.
