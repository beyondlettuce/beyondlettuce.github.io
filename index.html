Can we grow you something?

At Beyond Lettuce we provide chefs in the DC and N. VA with more than just mere greens. We grow indoors, year-round. That amazing malabar spinach is no longer available at your farmer's market? Let us know. Our faciliy is indoors and grows year-round. What can we grow for you?

How do we grow indoors?

The Beyond Lettuce farm.

We grow indoors in a warehouse setting using a combination of hydroponics and other indoor systems. We limit ourselves to produce that adapts well to an indoor environment. We supplement our light sources with the best grow light technology we can get. Our plant nutrients are developed in-house using worm compost and other organic sources.
